Perfectly positioned just south of downtown Stratford, Evanndor Place blends serene charm with prime convenience, resting a mere two blocks from the bustling main street and a short walk from the city’s most captivating attractions. Built in the 1860s, this historic treasure has been masterfully restored and thoughtfully modernized, its elegant furnishings a heartfelt nod to its rich past.

Please inquire by phone or email and allow us to help make your stay in Stratford enjoyable and memorable.
