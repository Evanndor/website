Below you will find Fequently Asked Questions. If you have a Question that is not answered here Please feel free to reach out and we would happily assist you.

  • Where is Evanndor Located?
    • Evanndor is a 5 minute walk from the Avon Theatre. You can find it on Google Maps Here

  • What is the best way to book with you?
    • Evanndor offers prefered pricing through private booking, however are on other booking platforms. Email correspondence is prefferd.

  • Does your facility have TV?
    • Ever room is equipped with a 40" TV as well as a Google Chromecast with Netflix. You will also have access to Local basic TV channels.

  • Is there internet available?
    • We have a 1Gigabit service which allows for all our guests to stream and use the internet without any problems.

  • Do all your rooms have a private bathroom?
    • Yes, ALL our rooms have their own designated bathrooms,they are not shared and are attached to the bedrooms.

  • How many guests can stay in each room?
    • Our main level rooms can accomodate 2 guests per room (Captain and Courtyard rooms). As for our upper level rooms (Sunset and Watercolour) 3 guests are permitted to ocupy the space. These rooms have a couch that converts to a sleeping arrangment

  • What forms of payment do you accept?
    • For our Canadian guests, we are currently accepting e-mail money/Interac money transfers, Cash or cheques. For our American guests we are accepting cash and checks. US cash will be converted to Canadian dollars based on the exchange rate on Google on the day of booking.

    We are currently not taking any Credit cards, however we are in the works of adding this as a payment method.

  • What do you request for a Depoist?
    • We request guests place a $200 deposit. This will be fully refunded if our guests cancel within 15 of check-in. If you cancel within the 15 day period your deposit will be forfeited. Bookings that are longer than 5 days will require a deposit that is 50% of the total booking cost including taxes.

    The remaining 50% will be due when guests arrive to check-in.

  • Can I buy gift certificate?
    • No. We do not offer gift certificates.

  • Can I arrive early?
    • Yes, we will do our bests to accomodate an early arrival, we request you inform us of the time you intend to arrive and we can potentially get you into your room earlier, however this is not always possible. We do accomodatge guests who arrive before check-in time by offering a place to store your belongings and offer parking (Subject to availablity). Standard check-in time is 4:00PM and checkout is 10:30AM

  • Can I request a late check-out?
    • You must inquire with us about a later check-out time. In the event there is no guest checking into your room we will accomodate a later depature time.

  • Can I stay for just one night?
    • Typically we request guests book a minimum of two nights. However if you're not booking a weekend stay there is the oppertuity to book a one night stay during the weeknights later in the season(July,August).

  • Do you serve breakfast?
    • We do not offer any breakfast, however every room has a bowl of fruit and some granola bars. There is also a complimentary bottle of wine in the fridge as well as bottles of water and milk for your coffee.

  • What appliances are in the room?
    • Each room is equiped with a K-cup coffee maker, electric kettle, microwave, and bar fridge. Courtyard is equipped witha full sized kitchen.

  • Is there parking available?
    • There is one spot deisgnated for each room, however if guests require even more parking there is a public lot located directly accross downie street at the bus terminal wich allows for overnight parking at no charge. Please inquire if you are in need of more parking as we may be able to accomodate that on site.

  • Can I bring my child?
    • Our facilities are not designed to accomodate small children.

  • Can I smoke or Vape in my room?
    • Smoking and Vaping in our facility is stictly prohibited. Outdoor spaces is permitted for smoking. Please be conscious of other guests who may have sensitivites. Smoking or vaping in an indoor room will result in a additional $300 surcharge in order to cover the cleaning of odours.

  • Can I bring my pet?
    • No.

  • Can you help with with restaruant suggestions/ Dinner reservations?
    • Most restaruants have an online reservation system on their websites, we recommend checking them out online as you can also see their menu. We do compile a list of restaruants that we update annualy, please inquire and we'd happiliy pass along any information we have.

  • Does the room require a key? Where do I pick up the key?
    • All our rooms, and entrance, have a keypad combonation to get into the facility. You will recieve an e-mail, or preffered correspondence with the details to gain access to your room. Generally these details will be sent off as soon as the room becomes available for you to check in.
