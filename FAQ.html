Below you will find Fequently Asked Questions. If you have a Question that is not answered here Please feel free to reach out and we would happily assist you.

  • Where is Evanndor Located?
    • Evanndor is a 5 minute walk from the Avon Theatre. You can find it on Google Maps Here

  • What is the best way to book with you?
    • Evanndor offers prefered pricing through private booking, however are on other booking platforms. Email correspondence is prefferd.

  • Does your facility have TV?
    • Ever room is equipped with a 40" TV as well as a Google Chromecast with Netflix. You will also have access to Local basic TV channels.

  • Is there internet available?
    • We have a 1Gigabit service which allows for all our guests to stream and use the internet without any problems.

  • Do all your rooms have a private bathroom?
    • Yes, ALL our rooms have their own designated bathrooms,they are not shared and are attached to the bedrooms.

  • How many guests can stay in each room?
    • Our main level rooms can accomodate 2 guests per room (Captain and Courtyard rooms). As for our upper level rooms (Sunset and Watercolour) 3 guests are permitted to ocupy the space. These rooms have a couch that converts to a sleeping arrangment

  • What forms of payment do you accept?
    • For our Canadian guests, we are currently accepting e-mail money/Interac money transfers, Cash or cheques. For our American guests we are accepting cash and checks. US cash will be converted to Canadian dollars based on the exchange rate on Google on the day of booking.

    We are currently not taking any Credit cards, however we are in the works of adding this as a payment method.

  • What do you request for a Depoist?
    • We request guests place a $200 deposit. This will be fully refunded if our guests cancel within 15 of check-in. If you cancel within the 15 day period your deposit will be forfeited. Bookings that are longer than 5 days will require a deposit that is 50% of the total booking cost including taxes.

    The remaining 50% will be due when guests arrive to check-in.

  • Can I buy gift certificate?
    • No. We do not offer gift certificates.

  • Can I arrive early?
    • Yes, we will do our bests to accomodate an early arrival, we request you inform us of the time you intend to arrive and we can potentially get you into your room earlier, however this is not always possible. We do accomodatge guests who arrive before checkin time by offering a place to store your belongings and offer parking (Subject to availablity).
